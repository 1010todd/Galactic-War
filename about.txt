Galactic War mod made by 1010todd

This mod is about an intergalactic war at a nearby galaxy, the story is currently being written.

This mod add several new ships and outfits most of them might be unbalanced yet, any feedback is appreciated.

To enter this mod's galaxy the wormhole is above the Wanderer space near the Pug system, make sure your ship(s) are at least tier 3 or higher as the enemy you're facing there are very powerful.(OP ships are also recommended)
